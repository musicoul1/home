Refund Policy

Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

We strive to provide high-quality products and services. However, we understand that there may be instances where you may need a refund. Please review our refund policy below:

Eligible for Refund

  • Incorrect or Damaged Products: If you receive an incorrect or damaged product, please contact our customer support within 2 days of receiving the order. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
  • Unfulfilled Orders: If your order is not fulfilled within the specified timeframe, we will issue a full refund.

Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of Mind: Once an order is placed and processed, we cannot accept returns or refunds due to a change of mind.
  • Digital Products: Digital products, such as online courses or downloadable content, are non-refundable.

Refund Process

  1. Contact Customer Support: Please contact our customer support team at musicoul1@gmail.com to initiate the refund process.
  2. Provide Order Information: Provide your order number and a brief explanation for the refund request.
  3. Verification: We will verify your order details and the reason for the refund.
  4. Refund Processing: Once your refund request is approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method.

Please note: Refund processing times may vary depending on your payment method and bank processing times.
