Course Summary

Welcome to the Indian Classical Music Course! Here are some important notes to help you understand the videos:

  • Episode 1: Introduction to Indian Classical Music
    Learn about the rich history and traditions of Indian Classical Music, its unique elements, and its significance in Indian culture.
  • Episode 2: What is Swar? How to sing Swar?
    Understand the concept of Swar (musical notes), their types, and how to produce them accurately. ¬†Practice exercises for singing Swar.
  • Episode 3: What is Alankaar? How to sing Alankaar?
    Discover Alankaars (ornamentations), their importance in adding beauty to the music, and techniques for singing them effectively.
  • Episode 4: What is Raag? Characteristics of Raag
    Explore the concept of Raag, the melodic framework in Indian Classical Music, and its essential characteristics.
  • Episode 5: What is Raag Vargikaran?
    Learn about the classification of Raags into different Thaats (scales) and understand the relationships between them.
  • Episode 6: What is Taal? All About Teen-taal
    Understand the concept of Taal (rhythm), learn about Teen-taal (a common rhythmic cycle), and practice counting and clapping Taals.

For more detailed notes and exercises, please refer to the full course materials.
